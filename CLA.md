## Why a CLA?

The shellkeep project is licensed under GPL-3.0-or-later. The CLA
preserves the option to relicense the project in the future if needed
(for example, to a different open source license). This is standard
practice for many open source projects.

**Transparency commitment:** Any relicensing decision will be:

- Communicated publicly with at least 90 days notice
- Limited to OSI-approved open source licenses
- Explained with clear rationale

The CLA will never be used to make the project proprietary or closed source.

## How to Sign

The CLA is managed via [CLA-assistant](https://cla-assistant.io/). When
you open your first pull request, the CLA-assistant bot will comment with
a link to review and sign the agreement electronically.

Signing is a one-time process. Once signed, all your future contributions
are covered.
